# Design System: Deep Sea Professional
 
## Visual Language
 
### Core Philosophy
This design system balances professional authority with a modern, approachable interface. By combining a deep, trustworthy teal with moderate corner rounding, the system achieves a balanced, secure aesthetic suitable for complex data environments or high-end professional tools.
 
### Color Palette
*   **Primary Color**: #005C72 (Deep Teal) – Used for primary actions, active states, and brand identity.
*   **Neutral Palette**: #F8FAFB (Cool Slate) – A crisp, cool-toned neutral used for backgrounds and surfaces to maintain a clean, airy feel.
*   **Color Mode**: Light mode is the primary orientation, prioritizing clarity and high-contrast readability.
 
### Typography
*   **Headlines**: Manrope – A modern geometric sans-serif that provides a clean, technological edge to titles and headers.
*   **Body & Labels**: Inter – Optimized for screen readability, ensuring that functional text and data remains legible at all sizes.
 
### Shape and Spacing
*   **Roundedness**: Moderate (Level 2). The interface utilizes a standard rounded design language. Buttons, input fields, and card containers feature noticeably softened corners, creating a modern and professional appearance without being overly organic.
*   **Spacing**: Normal (Level 2). The layout follows standard density patterns, providing enough breathing room for clarity without sacrificing information density.
 
## Component Specifications
*   **Buttons**: Moderately rounded edges with deep teal backgrounds.
*   **Form Fields**: Subtle borders with standard corner rounding to match the button styling.
*   **Cards**: Soft, rounded containers that leverage the cool neutral background to separate content sections.
